```Game of Trust```


To implement OOP Concept in a simple recreation on ‘The Evolution of Trust’ by Nicky Case, based off ‘The Evolution of Cooperation’ by Robert Axelrod in 1984 and it’s sequel ‘The Complexity of Cooperation’ in 1997.

Known in game theory, The Prisoner’s Dilemma, where two suspects can either expose their partner-in-crime (“cheat”), or stay silent (“cooperate”).


The prisoner’s dilemma provides a framework on understanding how to strike a balance between cooperation and competition.

To allow user to learn about the concepts of prisoner’s dilemma in which:
- two individual acting in their own self-interests do not produce the optimal outcome
- Mere cooperation is not always in one’s best interest
